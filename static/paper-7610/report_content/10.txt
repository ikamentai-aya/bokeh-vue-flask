trends, and spatial distribution of various attributes. We demonstrate
the functionality of S ET-S TAT-M AP using real-life datasets.
Although S ET-S TAT-M AP is currently a research prototype, we
plan to make our web-based implementation available to the developers through open-source software repositories. We believe
our approach to visualizing mixed data will inspire future work in
visualization and interface design to explore heterogeneous datasets.
